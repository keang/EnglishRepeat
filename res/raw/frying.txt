She poured a little vegetable oil into the frying pan. 
Then she turned on the stove. 
She took an egg out of the refrigerator. 
She cracked the egg into a small bowl. 
She put the eggshells into the kitchen trash bag. 
She waited for the oil to get hot. 
She poured the raw egg into the pan. 
After about two minutes, 
she flipped the egg over 
and cooked it for another two minutes. 
She turned off the stove and took the fried egg out of the pan. 
She put the egg on a small plate. 
She sprinkled pepper and salt on the egg. 
She took a fork out of the kitchen drawer. 
She walked over to the dining room table with her fork and plate. 
She tasted the egg. It was delicious.
